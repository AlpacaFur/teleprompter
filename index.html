This year we revamped our electrical power distribution system, replacing much of our old wiring with custom PCBs. Our central power distribution board features custom regulators for our low voltage components, such as the Jetson Orin Computer and PCIE card. The new Arm, Drive, and Radio Power boards further simplify the wiring for their respective subsystems. A breakout board for a new MA600 magnetic encoder has been created to more precisely determine the arm's position.
